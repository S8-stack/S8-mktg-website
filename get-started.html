Pre-requisites

Stack8 is bundled in a simple zip containing all modules and ressources for an easy startup. Virtually nothing more is required other than an IDE and the S8 stack that you can download below.

Download Stack8 v0.8 See Doc of Stack8 v0.8

Annotations
