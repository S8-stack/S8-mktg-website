A Solution for Every Stage

We provide a wide range of plans to help covering all your development phases.

We divide the plans in two groups. The self hosted plans provide a very fine grain control over the costs, which might interest organizations at any stage. The very production ready nature of the stack makes it possible to envision data center level with limited dev-ops overhead, at record low costs.

Cloud solutions provide a different angle of attack: rely on top level hardware (We mean, really top level) and top tier datacenters location to have your app delivering with virtually zero overhead on your side.
